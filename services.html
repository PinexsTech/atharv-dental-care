Our Dental Services

What We Offer

Comprehensive Dental Care Under One Roof

At Atharva Dental Clinic, we offer a full spectrum of dental treatments using the latest technology for the best results, delivered with gentleness and care.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements. Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone and topped with a crown for a complete smile restoration.

Root Canal Treatment

Save your natural tooth with a painless root canal procedure. We remove infected pulp, clean the root canals, and seal the tooth to stop pain and prevent further infection.

Teeth Cleaning (Scaling)

Professional scaling and polishing removes plaque, tartar and surface stains. Regular cleaning prevents cavities, gum disease and keeps your teeth looking their best.

Teeth Whitening

Brighten your smile with our professional teeth whitening treatments. We offer safe, effective whitening procedures that can lighten teeth up to several shades in a single session.

Braces Treatment

Correct misaligned teeth and jaw problems with traditional metal braces or ceramic braces. We create custom treatment plans to give you the straight smile you deserve.

Tooth Extraction

When a tooth cannot be saved, we perform gentle extractions. We use local anaesthesia to ensure the procedure is as comfortable as possible, including wisdom tooth removals.
